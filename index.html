About Dog Company

Dog company is a smaller Arma 3 community with a focus on tactical cooperative missions that play to the strengths of smaller units.

We are all adults and as such have real life responsibilities, we take a life comes first attitude to your attendance.

Every operation we play is custom built for us by our team of mission makers. There are no set loadouts or slots for members, however we do try to accommodate preferences where possible.

We are multicultural, including members from across Europe and the US, but that doesn't mean we are a "safe space" and we do not intend to become one. You need to be able to give as good as you get with us.
